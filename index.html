Tell Me More

Who We Are

Urban Arcadia.

We are a small furniture design company that focuses on beautiful rustic appeal with a modern functional twist. We pride ourselves on thinking outside the box to create unique quality pieces of decor that break the mold of those portrayed by large furniture companies.

Drawing inspiration from the old we believe beauty lies in the connections, materials, and adaptive re-use, where the character of the past adapts to our contemporary needs.

All our products are custom made to order and are designed to withstand the wear and tear of day to day use. Pieces differ depending on the availability of materials. To submit custom furniture requests and exact item specifications please contact us through our contact page.
